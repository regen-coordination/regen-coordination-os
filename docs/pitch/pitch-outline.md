# Coop Pitch Outline

## What

Coop is a browser-native knowledge commons for local and digital communities.

## Why

Communities lose context across tabs, calls, and chats, creating coordination drag.

## How

- Chromium extension + mobile PWA
- Local-first + P2P membrane + Filecoin archive
- Anchor nodes for stronger AI inference
- On-chain Coop registry and constrained agent wallets

## Now

- MVP covers impact reporting, coordination, governance, and capital formation.
- Telegram/BlueSky integrations intentionally deferred.

## Next

- Expand interoperability with Green Goods and regen-coordination-os.
- Add richer graph-based ecosystem relationships.
