# Ethereum Localism Strategy — GG24 / DDA Direction

Date: 2026-03-06  
Domain: regenerative-finance / local-governance  
Status: pipeline (GG24)

---

## What

Ethereum Localism is positioned as a high-leverage domain for Gitcoin 3.0 because it connects Ethereum infrastructure to local communities and economies, instead of relying on speculative adoption alone.

This strategy frames local deployment as the path to durable adoption by embedding:

- local coordination and governance tools;
- stablecoin and payment rails;
- local funding and capital formation mechanisms;
- reusable open-source playbooks that can be adapted across regions.

Source: `03 Libraries/Regen Coordination/Regen Coordination Docs/Ethereum Localism x Regen Coordination Gitcoin 3.0 Sensemaking Report DRAFT.txt`

---

## Why

The sensemaking report identifies two converging conditions:

1. Local and institutional systems are under pressure (economic volatility, ecological stress, and weak public service capacity), increasing demand for resilient local coordination infrastructure.
2. Ethereum UX and accessibility have improved enough that the bottleneck is now real-world utility and locally grounded product-market fit.

The strategic thesis is that meaningful mass adoption will come from "cosmo-local" integration: global protocol infrastructure combined with local implementation and trust networks.

---

## How

### Two integration fronts

1. **Infrastructural integration**: local communities use Ethereum tools for governance, coordination, and resource stewardship.
2. **Financial integration**: local economies use onchain payments, stablecoins, grants, DeFi, and impact-linked capital flows.

### Coordination model

- Regen Coordination acts as a network-of-networks layer linking ReFi DAO, GreenPill, Bloom, BioFi, and aligned partners.
- Local rounds are treated as structured experiments that generate evidence, methods, and reusable templates.

### Measurement model

- Track outcomes against RC north stars, including Total Value Flowed (TVF), adoption, and local ecological/social/economic outcomes.
- Use stack components such as Karma GAP, AI evaluation flows, and ecosystem-specific reporting methods.

## GG24 Domain Proposal Direction

The report proposes a co-design path with Gitcoin and OpenCivics toward a **Dedicated Domain Allocation (DDA)** for Ethereum Localism.

Planned direction includes:

- Regen Coordination and OpenCivics as founding stewards in early domain formation;
- 3-5 local pilot rounds in GG24;
- progressive decentralization of governance through peer validation;
- protocolized scaling target of 10+ rounds in 2026.

Fundraising reality in the report:

- Domain size expectation supports a $50k+ threshold.
- CeloPG budget request up to $75k is cited as pending community vote.
- Additional expected sponsor pathways include Ethereum ecosystem and aligned partner treasuries.
