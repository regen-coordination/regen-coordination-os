# Regen Toolkit Integration

## What it is

- Curated educational content repository for regenerative practitioners entering (or already in) Web3.
- Three tracks: Foundations, Applied Web3 for Impact, Playbooks/Patterns/Case Studies.
- Target audiences: Grounded Regen, Curious Degen, On-Chain Regen.

## Why it matters for regen-coordination-os

- High-value source for narrative framing, methodology, and ecosystem-level strategy.
- Contains actionable frameworks for impact evaluation, cosmo-local coordination, and local funding round design.
- Feeds node onboarding and capacity-building across the network.

## Integration modes

- **Content:** extract reusable frameworks, definitions, and process patterns into network knowledge commons.
- **Taxonomy:** align terms like ImpactQF, CIDS/Common Approach, Ethereum Localism, TVF, local rounds.
- **Workflow:** periodic synthesis from regen-toolkit into concise network artifacts; toolkit pulls from Regen Coordination strategy docs.
- **Technical:** structured extraction for indexing and cross-linking with knowledge domains.

## Suggested sync cadence

- **Monthly** strategic sync.
- **Event-driven** updates after major round retrospectives or methodology changes.

## Risks/blockers

- Long-form narrative format can make extraction inconsistent without templates.
- Some claims are campaign/retrospective-context specific and need careful contextualization.
- Cross-doc terminology can shift over time.
