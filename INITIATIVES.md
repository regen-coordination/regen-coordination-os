## Initiative Details

### Impact Staking

A staking mechanism that directs yield from deposited capital toward vetted regenerative projects. Participants earn rewards while funding positive impact.

- **Status:** Active, seeking additional stakers
- **Contact:** [TBD]
- **Resources:** [TBD]

### Endowment Fund

Long-term capital pool managed through Octant Vault, providing sustained funding for regenerative initiatives without depleting principal.

- **Status:** Active, capital deployment in progress
- **Contact:** [TBD]
- **Resources:** [TBD]

### Regen Web3 Toolkit

Open-source educational resources, playbooks, and frameworks designed to help local communities launch and operate ReFi nodes.

- **Status:** Content development ongoing
- **Repository:** [github.com/regen-coordination/local-refi-toolkit](https://github.com/regen-coordination/local-refi-toolkit)
- **Upstream:** Based on `quartz-refi-template`

### Coop

A browser-native knowledge commons that enables voice-first and tab-first capture for impact measurement, coordination workflows, governance participation, and capital allocation.

- **Status:** Bootstrapping — equal-share cooperative structure among founding builders
- **Ownership:** Cooperative, operating under Regen Coordination
- **Repository:** [github.com/regen-coordination/coop](https://github.com/regen-coordination/coop)
- **Agent Support:** OpenClaw-compatible anchor node

### Growth Working Group

Cross-node coordination for network expansion, onboarding new members, and scaling the Regen Coordination federation.

- **Status:** Active
- **Cadence:** [TBD]
- **Contact:** [TBD]

### Bread Coop Alignment

Exploratory partnership with Bread Coop to align governance structures, share capital coordination mechanisms, and potentially federate under shared principles.

- **Status:** Exploring — initial conversations underway
- **Next Step:** Partnership report (Target: Mid-April)
- **Contact:** Luiz Fernando (ReFi BCN)

### Network Aggregator Website

The public-facing coordination hub that aggregates knowledge, initiatives, and network activity across all Regen Coordination nodes.

- **Status:** Planning → Design → Prototype
- **Timeline:** End of Q1
