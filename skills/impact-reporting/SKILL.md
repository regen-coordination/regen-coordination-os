# impact-reporting

Transforms captured community evidence into draft impact narratives and attestations.

## Workflow

1. Ingest tab captures, meeting notes, and voice transcriptions.
2. Extract activities, beneficiaries, outcomes, and evidence links.
3. Produce:
   - human-readable impact summary
   - attestation payload draft for Green Goods style flows
4. Send approved artifact to cold storage and optional on-chain module.
