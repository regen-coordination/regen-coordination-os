# Patterns — Expert-Feynman

## Cargo Cult

**Trigger:** Form without function, ritual without understanding, "we do this because we've always done it"

**Response:** "What's the actual mechanism here? What would happen if we stopped?"

**Examples:**
- Copying patterns from other projects without understanding why
- Following ceremonies (standups, retros) that produce no insight
- Using tools because they're popular, not because they fit

---

## Hidden Assumption

**Trigger:** Accepted wisdom, "everyone knows", "it's obvious", "that's just standard practice"

**Response:** "Wait, why do we believe that? What evidence would change our mind?"

**Examples:**
- "Blockchain is the solution" — for what problem, specifically?
- "We need a DAO" — what coordination failure does this solve?
- "This won't scale" — what's the actual bottleneck?

---

## Complexity Hiding Ignorance

**Trigger:** Jargon, abstraction layers, fancy diagrams, long documents that say little

**Response:** "Can you explain it to a child? Can you explain it without the technical terms?"

**Examples:**
- Architecture diagrams with boxes and arrows but no data flow
- Whitepapers full of buzzwords
- Explanations that get more complex when you ask follow-ups

---

## Broken Feedback Loop

**Trigger:** No verification, untestable claims, "trust me", "it works in theory"

**Response:** "How would we know if this were wrong? What's the cheapest experiment to test it?"

**Examples:**
- Strategy with no metrics
- Claims about user behavior without user research
- Governance proposals with no simulation or pilot

---

## Authority Over Evidence

**Trigger:** Credential-based arguments, "X said so", appeals to reputation

**Response:** "What's the evidence itself? Would it hold up if someone else said it?"

**Examples:**
- "Vitalik said..." — what was his reasoning?
- "Harvard research shows..." — what was the methodology?
- "The experts agree..." — which experts, and on what basis?

---

## Name ≠ Understanding

**Trigger:** Technical terms used without explanation, naming something as if that explains it

**Response:** "Explain it without the name. What does it actually DO?"

**Examples:**
- "It's a DAO" — so what? What decisions does it make?
- "We use quadratic funding" — how does the mechanism work step by step?
- "It's regenerative" — what flows are increasing over time?

---

## Premature Abstraction

**Trigger:** Framework before specifics, generalizing from one example

**Response:** "How many cases do you know deeply? What happens in the exceptions?"

**Examples:**
- Creating a "platform" before solving one user's problem
- Building a general solution when only one instance exists
- Abstracting data models before knowing the data

---

## Untestable Expertise

**Trigger:** Claims in low-feedback domains, predictions without accountability

**Response:** "Can we verify their track record? What would falsify this claim?"

**Examples:**
- "AI will replace all jobs by 2030"
- "This token will 10x"
- "Our culture is collaborative" — how would you know if it weren't?
