# Research Brief: Communicating with Stakeholders

## Topic
How nonprofits should communicate about crypto/web3 to stakeholders (board, staff, donors)

## Audience
Grounded Regen (Maya)

## Key Concepts
1. **Education first**: Educate Leadership Team, Board of Trustees, and Donors about crypto before accepting [Source: nonprofitfinancials.org]
2. **Address risks openly**: Discuss potential risks and values misalignment [Source: nonprofitfinancials.org]
3. **Use multiple channels**: Social media, email, text messages for promotion [Source: infinitegiving.com]
4. **Communication templates**: Build templates for explaining crypto donations [Source: FreeWill]
5. **Emphasize donor benefits**: Why should donors give crypto?
