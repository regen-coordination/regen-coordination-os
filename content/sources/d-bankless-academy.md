# Bankless Academy

Bankless Academy provides interactive, beginner-friendly lessons for learning web3 fundamentals. It's one of the most polished educational resources in the ecosystem.

## Overview

Built by Bankless, the Academy offers a structured learning path for newcomers to web3. The lessons are interactive, often including quizzes and practical exercises that help solidify understanding.

## Key Sections

- **Web3 Basics**: Foundational concepts and terminology
- **Wallet Setup**: Practical guides for wallet creation and security
- **DeFi Fundamentals**: Introduction to decentralized finance
- **NFTs and DAOs**: Understanding digital ownership and coordination
- **Security**: Protecting yourself in web3

## When to Reference

- Onboarding complete beginners to web3
- Need interactive learning resources
- Looking for polished, user-tested educational content
- Want to link to trusted external education

## Content Highlights

The Academy's strength is its accessibility and production quality. Lessons are designed for people with no prior crypto knowledge and include practical exercises.

For Regen Toolkit content targeting beginners, Bankless Academy lessons can serve as recommended prerequisites or supplementary learning. Link to specific lessons when covering topics they address well.
