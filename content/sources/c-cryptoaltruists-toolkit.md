# CryptoAltruists Web3 Impact Toolkit

The CryptoAltruists Web3 Impact Toolkit is an upcoming resource specifically designed for nonprofits and impact organizations looking to leverage web3 technology safely and effectively.

## Overview

CryptoAltruists has been working with nonprofits to bridge the gap between traditional impact work and web3 opportunities. Their toolkit focuses on practical implementation with a strong emphasis on safety and risk management.

## Key Sections (Expected)

- **Nonprofit Onboarding**: Safe entry points for traditional organizations
- **Practical How-Tos**: Step-by-step guides for common operations
- **Safety Guidelines**: Risk management and security best practices
- **Fundraising**: Web3-native fundraising mechanisms for nonprofits
- **Compliance**: Navigating regulatory considerations
